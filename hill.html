Hill View Park

Hill View Park is as stunning as it is well-maintained. It stands at about 350ft from Idukki dam and is situated almost a kilometre away from the center of Idukki. Hill View Park, as its name implies, is one of the locations in Idukki that provides grand views of the Cheruthoni and Idukki dams. Hill View Park also has dining options and restrooms. The park's allure is enhanced by the presence of a natural lake.
